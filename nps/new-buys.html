Key Takeaway

NPS made a strong bet on digital streaming (Spotify, $316M) and construction materials (CRH, $258M). The fund also expanded into cybersecurity (CyberArk), space technology (Rocket Lab), and fintech (SoFi), signaling diversification into high-growth sectors.

What the New Buys Tell Us

AI Infrastructure Play

Several new positions (Astera Labs, Credo Technology, CoreWeave, Ciena, Teradyne) are directly tied to AI data center buildouts. NPS is betting that the AI infrastructure wave still has significant runway.

Consumer Technology

The $316M Spotify position is the largest new buy by far. NPS appears to be confident in Spotify's expanding margins and dominant position in audio streaming after the company's strong profitability turnaround.

Emerging Market Fintech

Small but notable positions in StoneCo (Brazil) and Grab (Southeast Asia) show NPS exploring fintech opportunities in high-growth emerging markets.
