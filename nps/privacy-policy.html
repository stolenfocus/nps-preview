Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 20, 2026

Who We Are

KStockGuide ("we", "our", "us") operates kstockguide.com, a website that tracks institutional investors' US stock portfolios through publicly available SEC 13F filings. We do not require user registration and do not collect personal information such as names, email addresses, or financial data.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information directly. However, third-party services integrated into our site may collect certain data automatically:

  • Google Analytics — collects anonymized usage data such as pages visited, time on site, browser type, device type, and approximate geographic location. This helps us understand how visitors use our site.
  • Microsoft Clarity — records anonymized session replays and heatmaps to help us improve site usability. No personally identifiable information is collected.
  • Google AdSense — may serve personalized or non-personalized ads and use cookies to track ad performance. Google's advertising cookies enable it and its partners to serve ads based on visits to this and other websites.

Cookies

Our site uses cookies through the third-party services listed above. Cookies are small text files stored on your device. They are used to:

  • Analyze site traffic and usage patterns (Google Analytics)
  • Understand user interactions (Microsoft Clarity)
  • Serve relevant advertisements (Google AdSense)
  • Remember your theme preference (dark/light mode)

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some site functionality.

Google AdSense & Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. For more information, see How Google uses information from sites that use its services.

Third-Party Links

Our site contains links to external websites such as SEC EDGAR. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party sites.

Data Retention

We do not store any user data on our servers. All analytics data is retained by the respective third-party service providers according to their own data retention policies.

Children's Privacy

Our site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our GitHub repository.
