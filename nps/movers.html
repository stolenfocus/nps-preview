Reading Between the Lines

Conviction in Streaming & SaaS

The massive Netflix (+911%) and ServiceNow (+512%) increases suggest NPS is making a major bet on recurring-revenue business models. Both companies have demonstrated pricing power and improving margins.

Reducing Cyclical Exposure

The sell-down of Weyerhaeuser (-97%), Coterra Energy (-78%), and Corteva (-55%) indicates a deliberate reduction in exposure to commodity-sensitive and cyclical sectors.

Value Opportunity in Autos

The +79% increase in General Motors ($317M total) is notable — NPS may see the automaker as undervalued relative to its EV transition progress and strong cash flow generation.
